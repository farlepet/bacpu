AND instruction
---------------

    Ands two registers.

*** Instruction Flow ***

AND
    * Check that both registers are valid
        * False
            * Increase PC
            * Call exception
        * True
            * Set ALU to AND
            * AND two registers
            * Set first register to the result
            * Increase PC


*** Instruction Format ***

	Instruction Format: OpCode with two registers
	First Register:     Value 1 and Result
	Second Register:    Value 2
